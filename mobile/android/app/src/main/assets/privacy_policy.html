Privacy Policy for FocusMother

Last Updated: January 11, 2026
Effective Date: January 11, 2026

Introduction

FocusMother ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Android mobile application.

By using FocusMother, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

App Usage Statistics (Local Only):

  • Screen time duration per app
  • App package names and categories
  • App usage timestamps
  • Agreement compliance history
Important: Usage statistics are stored ONLY on your device and are NEVER sent to our servers or third parties.

1.2 Information You Provide

Selfie Photo (Avatar Creation):

  • When you create a personalized avatar, you may choose to provide a selfie photo
  • This photo is sent to Ready Player Me (our third-party avatar creation service)
  • The photo is used solely to generate your 3D avatar and is not stored by FocusMother

AI Conversation Messages:

  • Text messages you send during AI conversations with your digital guardian
  • These messages are sent to Anthropic (our AI service provider) to generate responses
  • Conversations are stored locally on your device for your reference

Optional Information:

  • Daily screen time goals
  • Custom app categorization preferences
  • Quiet hours preferences

1.3 Technical Information

Device Information:

  • Android version
  • Device model (for crash reporting)
  • App version
  • Crash logs (if crash reporting is enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

We use collected information to:

  • Monitor your app usage and detect excessive screen time
  • Provide personalized AI-powered interventions through conversations
  • Create and display your personalized 3D avatar
  • Enforce negotiated time agreements
  • Display usage statistics and trends
  • Improve the accuracy of app categorization

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze crash reports to fix bugs and improve stability
  • Understand feature usage to prioritize improvements
  • Optimize AI conversation quality

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Services

Anthropic (Claude AI):

  • What we share: Your conversation messages and contextual usage information (app category, screen time duration - NOT specific app names)
  • Purpose: To generate AI responses and negotiate time agreements
  • Data retention: Anthropic retains data according to their privacy policy (https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
  • Location: Data processed in the United States

Ready Player Me:

  • What we share: Your selfie photo (only if you choose to create an avatar)
  • Purpose: To generate your personalized 3D avatar
  • Data retention: Ready Player Me retains data according to their privacy policy (https://readyplayer.me/privacy)
  • Location: Data processed in the European Union

Firebase Crashlytics (Optional):

  • What we share: Crash logs, device model, Android version
  • Purpose: To identify and fix app crashes
  • Data retention: 90 days
  • Opt-out: You can disable crash reporting in Settings

3.2 What We DO NOT Share

  • Specific app names you use (only categories like "Social Media" or "Games")
  • Exact screen time per app with third parties
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • All app usage statistics are stored ONLY on your device
  • Conversation history is stored locally in an encrypted database
  • Avatar files (3D model and thumbnail) are stored locally

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption at rest: API keys encrypted using Android KeyStore (AES-256-GCM)
  • Encryption in transit: All network communications use HTTPS with certificate pinning
  • Secure deletion: Biometric data (avatar files) is securely overwritten before deletion (DoD 5220.22-M standard)
  • No cloud backup: Sensitive data is excluded from Android cloud backups
  • Code obfuscation: Release builds use ProGuard to prevent reverse engineering

4.3 Data Retention

  • Conversation messages: Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Usage statistics: Retained indefinitely on your device (you can clear via Settings)
  • Avatar data: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • API keys: Retained until you remove them or uninstall the app

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • View all data: See all stored conversations, agreements, and usage statistics in the app
  • Delete data: Completely delete all data at any time via Settings → Delete All Data
  • Export data: Future feature - export your data in CSV format
  • Disable features: Turn off monitoring, avatar, or crash reporting at any time

5.2 Data Deletion

To delete ALL your data:

  1. Open FocusMother
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy & Data
  3. Tap "Delete All Data"
  4. Confirm deletion

This will:

  • Securely erase avatar files (GLB and thumbnail)
  • Delete all conversation history
  • Delete all usage statistics
  • Delete all agreements
  • Remove all settings and preferences

To completely remove all traces:

  • After deleting data, uninstall the app from your device

5.3 GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to data processing for certain purposes
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@focusmother.app (email to be created).
